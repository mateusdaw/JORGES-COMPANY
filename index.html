Our story

A decade of craft, brought to Massachusetts.

Jorge's Air Conditioning & Heating was founded by Fabiano Jorge José Júnior, a seasoned HVAC professional originally from São Paulo, Brazil. Fabiano brought a wealth of technical know-how and a genuine passion for comfort to Marlborough, where he's called home since 2019.

Every job — install, repair, or routine maintenance — is handled with the same attention to detail, whether it's a full system replacement or a seasonal tune-up. That commitment is what's turned first-time customers into long-term clients across the region.

Jorge's HVAC is also a proud Mass Save® partner, helping homeowners navigate rebates on high-efficiency system upgrades — often worth thousands of dollars back.
